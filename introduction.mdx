Explore guides and a variety of resources to help you get started adding live and on demand video experiences to your application using the open and decentralized Livepeer Protocol. Interested in participating in the network as an orchestrator or delegator? We got you covered too.

## Core concepts

Discover the 3 core pillars of Livepeer: Developers, Orchestrators and Delegators. Each one plays a vital role in Livepeer ecosystem.

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Developers" href="./concepts/spaces" icon="code">
    With just a few simple API calls, create unique live and on-demand video
    experiences that play anywhere and look beautiful, every time, at scale.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Orchestrators" href="./concepts/workbooks" icon="circle-nodes">
    You can become an orchestrator by running software that allows you to
    contribute your computer's resources in service of processing and
    distributing video for developers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delegators" href="./concepts/events" icon="coins">
    Delegators contribute to securing the network without running a Livepeer
    node themselves. Explore the curated set of delegator guides to get started.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
