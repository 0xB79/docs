Livepeer is a video infrastructure protocol powering the next generation of live and on-demand video experiences. Designed to give developers the freedom to innovate, creators autonomy from platforms, and viewers a choice in their experience.

Explore guides and a variety of resources to help you get started adding live and on demand video experiences to your application using the open and decentralized Livepeer Protocol. Interested in participating in the network as an orchestrator or delegator? We got you covered too.

## Core concepts

Discover the 3 core pillars of Livepeer: Developers, Orchestrators and Delegators. Each one plays a vital role in Livepeer ecosystem.
